1.0 Staff Rules

1.1 Personal Gain

  • Staff must not spawn items, money, vehicles, or any other assets for personal gain.
  • Any use of staff permissions must have a legitimate server or support reason behind it.

1.2 Conflict of Interest

  • If a staff member is conflicted in a situation, that situation must be passed to a non-conflicted staff member.
  • If no non-conflicted staff members are available, owners will take over and assist.

1.3 Owner Escalation

  • Do not ask the owners about every small issue.
  • Owners are there to support the staff team, but they also have their own responsibilities and tasks to complete.
  • Staff are expected to use judgment and handle situations within their role where appropriate.

1.4 Abuse of Powers

  • Staff must not abuse the admin powers they are given.
  • Any findings of staff power abuse may result in immediate removal from the staff team.

1.5 Higher Standard

  • Staff are held to a higher standard than the general player base.
  • If staff are found breaking general server rules, stricter levels of punishment can and will be applied.
