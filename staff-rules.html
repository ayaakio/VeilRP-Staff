1.0 Staff Rules

1.1 Personal Gain

Staff permissions must never be used for personal benefit.

  • No spawning items for personal gain.
  • No spawning money, vehicles, or assets for yourself or friends.
  • Any use of staff permissions must have a legitimate staff or support reason behind it.

1.2 Conflict of Interest

If a staff member is personally involved in a situation, they must step away from handling it.

  • If conflicted with a situation, the case must be passed to a non-conflicted member of staff.
  • If no non-conflicted staff are available, owners will take over and assist.
  • Do not make decisions in situations where your judgment may be biased.

1.3 Owner Escalation

Owners are there to support the staff team, not to handle every minor issue.

  • Do not ask the owners every little thing.
  • Owners are here to support, but they also have their own tasks and responsibilities.
  • Staff are expected to use judgment and handle routine situations within their role.

1.4 Abuse of Powers

Administrative powers are provided for server support and moderation only.

  • Do not abuse the admin powers you are given.
  • Any findings of power abuse may result in immediate removal from the staff team.
  • All staff actions should be explainable and justifiable if reviewed later.

1.5 Higher Standard

Staff are expected to represent the server and set an example for the community.

  • Staff are held to a higher standard than the general player base.
  • If staff are found breaking general rules, stricter punishment can and will be applied.
  • Staff should remain professional, fair, and consistent in all actions and interactions.
