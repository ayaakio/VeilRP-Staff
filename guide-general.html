1.0 General Staff Conduct

1.1 Overview

Staff are expected to represent the server at all times, both in-game and outside of the server.

Your behaviour reflects not only yourself, but the staff team and the server as a whole.

1.2 Professional Behaviour

Staff should always aim to act in a calm, fair, and professional manner.

  • Remain respectful in all interactions.
  • Avoid arguments or unnecessary confrontation.
  • Be clear and direct when speaking to players.
  • Do not act on emotion or frustration.
  • Maintain composure even in difficult situations.

1.3 In-Game Conduct

While in-game, staff should balance their role as both a player and a moderator.

  • Do not let staff duties interfere with ongoing roleplay unless necessary.
  • Avoid using staff powers in active scenes unless required.
  • Do not involve yourself in situations where you are personally involved.
  • Remain fair and neutral when handling in-game situations.
  • Ensure your actions support roleplay rather than disrupt it.

1.4 Out of Character Conduct

Staff behaviour outside of character is just as important as in-game behaviour.

  • Do not engage in toxicity, drama, or arguments.
  • Maintain a respectful tone in all OOC interactions.
  • Do not discuss sensitive staff matters publicly.
  • Keep communication clear, calm, and professional.
  • Be mindful of how you represent the server at all times.

1.5 Decision Making

Staff decisions should always be fair, consistent, and justifiable.

  • Take time to understand situations before acting.
  • Gather context where needed.
  • Avoid making rushed or emotional decisions.
  • Ensure actions are consistent with server expectations.
  • Be able to explain your reasoning if reviewed.

1.6 Communication

Clear communication is key when interacting with players and other staff.

  • Explain decisions when necessary.
  • Avoid vague or unclear responses.
  • Do not escalate situations through tone or wording.
  • Listen to players before responding.
  • Work with other staff members when needed.

1.7 Working as a Team

Staff should work together to maintain consistency and support each other.

  • Communicate with other staff when handling situations.
  • Do not override decisions without discussion.
  • Support team decisions even if you were not directly involved.
  • Ask for help when unsure.
  • Keep internal matters within the staff team.

2.0 Good Practice

2.1 Best Practice

  • Stay calm and composed at all times.
  • Be consistent in how you treat players.
  • Use the least intrusive action needed to resolve issues.
  • Focus on improving the player experience.
  • Act in the best interest of the server, not yourself.

3.0 What to Avoid

3.1 Poor Conduct

  • Do not engage in arguments with players.
  • Do not act out of frustration or anger.
  • Do not show bias or favouritism.
  • Do not abuse your position as staff.
  • Do not ignore issues that require attention.
